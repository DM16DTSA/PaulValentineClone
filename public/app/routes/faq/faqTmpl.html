Where is my package?

When you have completed your order you will receive a confirmation e-mail and, as soon as the shipment is created, an e-mail with your tracking information. All of our international orders are sent via DHL and can be traced via their homepage.

Where is my strap?

Orders only containing straps are sent via regular mail and may take up to 10 days to arrive. For these orders you will not receive a tracking number.

How long does the shipping usually take?

We strive to deliver worldwide within five days and within Europe even faster.
For an exact estimation proceed to the checkout and enter your address.

Where can you guys ship my watch?

We ship worldwide to every country. For free.

Can I change my order after it is completed?

Since we’re pretty quick at processing and packing your order, unfortunately no changes can be made after you’ve placed your order.

However, there might be enough time to cancel your order and create a new one. If you wish to do so, please submit a request with our customer support team via customercare@paul-valentine.com. We’ll cancel your order if it hasn’t been processed and packed yet.

CAN I RETURN THE WATCH IF I CHANGE MY MIND?

If you are not completely satisfied with your purchase, for any reason, we will offer you a full refund within 30 days of purchase.

In case the buyer wishes to return the product, he or she is allowed to unpack the product and only use it to determine whether he/she wants to keep the product. The returned products will be carefully examined, as they must be unworn and in their original packaging, containing all accessories of the packaging. Please note, that if a buyer wishes to return the order, the shipping cost is his/her responsibility. Please always contact our customer service via customercare@paul-valentine.com before you send anything back. Will instruct you how to proceed, thank you.

When do I receive the money back from my return?

We usually process refunds at the end of the same week we received your product.

My watch is defect, what should I do?

Your satisfaction is very important to us, please contact us at customercare@paul-valentine.com and include a picture or two of the defect. Please do not return your product without consulting our customer care.
