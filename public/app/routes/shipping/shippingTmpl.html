SHIPPING POLICY

We offer free standard shipping on all orders!

We aim to dispatch all orders within 1-3 business days from when the order was placed. Please allow 1-2 business days to receive your shipping confirmation and tracking information.

CUSTOMS, TAXES, DUTIES, AND FEES

You will be liable for all import duties, customs and taxes. These will be due at the point of, or after delivery.

Please be aware that Paul Valentine does not have any control for any duties, customs and taxes levied by the country your order is being shipped to. We cannot advise you what the cost will be as these charges can vary according to country.

Since these fees are not computed nor applied to orders by Paul Valentine, please contact your local customs office to find out more information if you are unsure of the charges.
